# University Recruiting Keynote

You're on the talent brand team building the keynote deck for a university recruiting tour this fall.

It needs to open with real visual presence — team and workplace photography rather than a text-heavy title page — and land a handful of headline numbers candidates actually care about: 40% growth in the engineering team this year, unlimited PTO, and a fully remote-first policy with quarterly in-person offsites. Spread a couple of real team photos through the deck rather than clustering them all up front, since the room's attention fades if the visual energy only shows up once. Close with a strong, direct call to apply, including the careers site URL.

Audience is graduating computer science students at a career fair — keep the tone energetic and a little bold, closer to a brand moment than a corporate benefits summary.
