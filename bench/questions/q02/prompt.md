# Aria Pro Launch Keynote

You're on the launch team for Aria Pro, a new wireless earbud shipping to consumer press next month. The keynote deck opens the press event, so it needs to feel like a launch moment, not a spec sheet: start big and visual, then land the headline numbers people will actually repeat afterward — 38-hour total battery life with the case, -32dB active noise cancellation, and a $179 price point that undercuts the category leader by $50.

Give the hardware itself real visual presence somewhere prominent using the attached photography rather than describing it purely in words, and pair a couple of feature highlights (the case's wireless charging, the new pressure-fit ear tips) with supporting imagery rather than a plain list. Close on the on-sale date (September 14) and a tagline moment the room will remember.

Audience is tech press and early-access customers, so tone should be confident and a little theatrical, not corporate.
