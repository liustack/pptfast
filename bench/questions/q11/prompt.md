# Open Enrollment Reference Guide

You're the benefits coordinator producing the detailed reference guide employees read on their own time before open enrollment runs November 1st through 15th — not a live presentation, a document people return to while they're deciding.

Cover the plan tiers on offer (an HSA-eligible high-deductible plan, a standard PPO, and a dental buy-up option) with what each actually costs per paycheck this year versus last year side by side, a careful walk-through of the enrollment process in the benefits portal from first login to confirmation email, and answers to the eight questions that flooded the HR inbox during last year's window — things like what happens to unused HSA funds, whether a life event outside the window qualifies for a special enrollment, and how dependents get added.

This needs to stand alone without anyone in the room to answer follow-up questions, so err toward more explanation rather than less, and make sure every plan-tier number is easy to find without hunting.
