# H2 Engineering Planning Review

You're the head of product presenting the second-half planning review to engineering leadership, organized by team — platform, growth, core product, and data — each needing to show their own upcoming sequence of bets.

The teams don't share a planning rhythm (platform works in two-week slices, data works in monthly ones), so what matters is that each team's own sequence of upcoming work reads clearly on its own, side by side with the others, rather than forcing everything onto one shared calendar it doesn't actually follow.

Because the eng leads want to work through the team groupings together in a session before content gets written, lay out the page structure first and bring it to that working session for their sign-off. After that, expect each team lead to send their own section's content back to you individually over the next week, so pages fill in incrementally rather than all at once — structure the work so that's easy to do without disturbing sections that are already finished. Treat the plan as pre-approved once it validates — no confirmation round trip is available.
