# Analytics Dashboard Launch Readiness Review

Product marketing is preparing an internal stakeholder walkthrough of the new analytics dashboard feature ahead of next week's launch readiness review — lower drama than a customer keynote, more information-dense, since the room already knows the product and wants specifics.

Each major capability (custom report builder, scheduled exports, the new anomaly-detection alerts) should get its own moment with a short, concrete benefit statement rather than being grouped into one generic features slide. Include the adoption numbers from the closed beta: a target of 500 workspaces opted in within the first 30 days of general availability, against 340 already opted in during the beta period, which is the number leadership will ask about first.

Close with a readiness checklist spanning engineering (load-tested at 3x expected traffic), support (playbook published), and marketing (launch email drafted) so the room can see at a glance whether anything is still blocking the go date.
