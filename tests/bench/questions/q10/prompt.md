# Brazil Market Entry Recommendation

Corp dev is recommending whether the company should enter the Brazilian market, presenting to the executive committee for a go/no-go decision next week.

The addressable market there is roughly $2.1B, growing at a 14% annual rate. Three entry paths are on the table — an acquisition of a regional player, a joint venture with an existing distributor, and a from-scratch direct launch — and they need to be scored against each other on speed to first revenue, regulatory exposure, and how much capital each one ties up, so the committee can see the trade-off at a glance rather than reading three separate write-ups. Separately, map where the three most credible competitors sit on cost position versus how broad their service offering is, since that's what determines whether there's real room to compete.

Close with an unmistakable recommendation — which path, and what needs to happen in the next 90 days if the committee says yes. Formal, board-ready, numbers-forward — this is a room that will push back on anything soft.
