# Annual Impact Report

You're the development director at a rural literacy nonprofit putting together the annual impact report for major donors ahead of the year-end giving season.

Open on the scale of the need in the communities the program serves, using the attached program photo rather than describing the setting in words alone. Move through this year's actual outcomes: 1,240 children enrolled across 14 new classroom sites opened this year, and an 89% improvement rate in measured reading level among children who completed a full year in the program. Include a moment built around what one participating family said, in their own words, about what changed for their child.

Close on next year's ask — an $850K target, with a clear breakdown of what the money funds (new sites, teacher stipends, materials). This needs to read warmly, like something a donor wants to keep rather than file away, even though the numbers still need to be easy to find and easy to trust.
